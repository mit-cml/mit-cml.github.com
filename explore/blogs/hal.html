MIT App Inventor Classic RIP (Jan. 2012 - Jul. 2015)

Submitted by hal on Wed, 07/15/2015 - 08:52

MIT App Inventor Classic RIP (Jan. 2012 - Jul. 2015). Today, July 15, 2015, we’re shutting down MIT App Inventor Classic. It’s been a great run. Even at the end, the system has been hosting 3000 active users a week. That’s down from 6500 in May, and vastly down from the peak of 30,000 active users a week in January 2014, when we first began encouraging people to move to App Inventor 2.
