App Inventor Summit @ MIT

July 13th & 14th, 2012

The MIT Center for Mobile Learning will host a two-day summit in Cambridge, MA. This meeting will allow time for small working groups to develop strategic plans for specific topics related to App Inventor. Invited presenters will share the work they have done with App Inventor.
